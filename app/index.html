Options

These fields connect Tsuki Hoshi feedback to your local LM Studio, Ollama, or OpenAI-compatible server.
Recommended: LM Studio or Ollama with a small instruct model. This feature only needs a small context window; use modern edge models around 1B-4B parameters or small quantizations that fit within about 4GB VRAM/RAM, such as Qwen, Gemma, Llama, Phi, or other efficient local assistant models.
